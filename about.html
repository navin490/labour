About Point Labour

What is Point Labour?

Point Labour is a web-based platform that helps contractors quickly find skilled and verified labourers like electricians, plumbers, carpenters, masons, and painters, based on location and availability. It aims to reduce the daily hustle of finding trusted workers for construction, maintenance, and home services.

Trust & Verification

Every labourer registering through Point Labour is required to submit valid documents like Aadhaar, PAN, and skill details. This helps contractors find trusted and skilled workers without worrying about fraud or inexperience. We do not store personal data publicly, ensuring your privacy is protected.
